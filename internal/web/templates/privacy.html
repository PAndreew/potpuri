Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

What Potpuri stores

Potpuri stores your email address, password hash, account settings, API token hashes, encrypted saved items, and encrypted uploaded file content.

Encryption

Saved item text and uploaded file content are encrypted before being stored. Search uses derived tokens so the database can match entries without storing plaintext search text.

Payments

If you become a Patron, payment processing is handled by the payment provider. Potpuri stores only the account status needed to enable Patron features.

No tracking or ads

Potpuri does not sell user data, serve ads, or include third-party analytics.

Deleting your data

You can delete your account from the account page. Deletion removes your account and saved data from Potpuri.
